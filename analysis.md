1. **Screen Resolution:**
   - Recommended specification: 4K (3840 x 2160 pixels) or higher
   - Suitable screen types: IPS, OLED, or QLED
   - Aspect ratio: 16:9

2. **Display Size:**
   - Preferred display sizes for various use cases:
     - Mobile devices: 6-7 inches
     - Tablets: 10-12 inches
     - Laptops: 13-15 inches
     - Gaming monitors: 24-34 inches
   - Screen ratio: 1-1.5:1 (portability) or 1.5-2:1 (gaming)

3. **Processor (CPU):**
   - Recommended processor architecture: ARM Cortex-A78 or higher (mobile) or Intel Core i5/i7 or higher (laptops)
   - Cores and threads: Quad-core, dual-cluster or higher (mobile) or quad-core/ hexa-core or higher (laptops)
   - Clock speed: 2GHz or higher

4. **Memory and Storage:**
   - Memory (RAM): 6GB or higher (mobile) or 8GB or higher (laptops/gaming)
   - Storage:
     - Mobile devices: 64GB or higher
     - Laptops: 256GB or higher
     - Gaming monitors: 1TB or higher

5. **Graphics Processing Unit (GPU):**
   - Recommended GPU type: Mali-G78 or Adreno 730 (mobile) or NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1650 or higher (laptops/gaming)
   - Integrated GPU: Intel Iris or UHD Graphics (mobile) or Intel HD Graphics 630 or higher (laptops)

6. **Battery Life:**
   - Average battery life for various use cases:
     - Mobile devices: 10-12 hours
     - Laptops: 8-12 hours
     - Gaming monitors: 10-15 hours

7. **Connectivity:**
   - Required wireless connectivity options:
     - Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) or higher
     - Bluetooth 5.0 or higher
     - NFC (optional)

8. **Operating System:**
   - Recommended operating system: latest Android (mobile) or latest Windows 10 (laptops)

9. **Biometric Authentication:**
   - Required biometric authentication methods: Fingerprint scanner (mobile) or facial recognition (laptops)
   - Optional biometric authentication methods: iris scanner, palm scanner, or vein scanner

10. **Charging and Power Consumption:**
    - Recommended charging method: USB-C, Quick Charge (mobile), or 120W fast charging (laptops)
    - Power consumption (Typical, Max): 3W (mobile), 25W (laptops), 50W (gaming monitors)

By providing these essential specifications aligned with user preferences, the chosen product can offer an optimal user experience.
